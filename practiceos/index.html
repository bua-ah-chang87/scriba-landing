How it works

Up and running
in three steps.

01
Enter client data

Paste raw meeting notes, upload a fact-find PDF, or fill in fields manually. Scriba extracts and structures the data automatically.

02

One click drafts all mandatory SOA sections simultaneously, with Australian regulatory standards built in and ready for adviser review.

Run an automated compliance check against AFSL obligations and Best Interests Duty. Edit any section, save versions, then export to Word or PDF.
